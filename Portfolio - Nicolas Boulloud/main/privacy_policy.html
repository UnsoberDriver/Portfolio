Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Data collected

When you use the contact form, the following information may be collected:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Message sent via the form

2. Purpose of collection

Data collected via the form is used solely to:

  • Respond to requests sent by the user.
  • Follow up on exchanges if necessary.

3. Legal basis for processing

Data processing is based on:

  • The legitimate interest of the site publisher in responding to requests and following up on exchanges.
  • The user's explicit consent when submitting information via the form.

4. Retention period

  • Data from the contact form: retained for a maximum of 3 years from the last exchange.

5. Data recipients

Data is intended solely for the site publisher, Nicolas Boulloud, and is never transferred or sold to third parties.

6. User rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability (if applicable)

To exercise your rights, you may send a request to the following address: Boulloud.nicolas@gmail.com. If, after contacting the publisher, you believe your rights are not being respected, you may file a complaint with the CNIL (www.cnil.fr).

7. Data security

The publisher implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against loss, unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

8. Cookies

This site does not use any tracking or advertising cookies. No automatic collection of personal data is performed beyond the information you voluntarily submit via the contact form.

9. Bot protection (Cloudflare Turnstile)

To protect the contact form against spam and automated abuse, this site uses Cloudflare Turnstile, a privacy-focused alternative to traditional CAPTCHAs. Turnstile runs invisibly and does not require any interaction from you. It processes minimal technical signals (such as IP address, browser information, and site metadata) solely to distinguish human visitors from bots. Cloudflare does not use this data for advertising purposes. For more information, please refer to Cloudflare's Turnstile Privacy Addendum.

10. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time to reflect legal or technical developments. The date of the last update is shown at the top of the page.
